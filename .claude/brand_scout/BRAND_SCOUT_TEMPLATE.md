# Brand Scout Report - Master Research Template

**Use this template for all new Brand Scout automated research leads.**

---

## Research Strategy (Execute in This Order)

1. **First Priority**: Search for official case studies, partnership announcements, and press releases about their fulfillment operations
2. **Second Priority**: Scrape shipping policy pages, contact pages, and help center pages directly
3. **Third Priority**: LinkedIn searches for warehouse/operations managers and C-suite executives
4. **Compile ALL findings** before responding in the structured format below

## Key Requirements Checklist

**Before submitting the report, verify**:

- [ ] **3PL Provider**: Specific company name found (e.g., "Red Stag Fulfillment" not just "they use a 3PL")
  - If not found, mark as ❌ in Section 4

- [ ] **Contact Info**: Official company emails preferred over personal emails
  - CEO/Founder contact confirmed
  - Operations contact confirmed (if exists)

- [ ] **Carriers**: Confirmed by scraping official help/shipping policy pages
  - Not assumed from generic industry knowledge

- [ ] **Performance Data**: Customer review patterns analyzed
  - Shipping complaints identified
  - Delivery time feedback compiled

- [ ] **Confidence Levels**: All data points marked with ✅ ⚠️ or ❌
  - Sources provided for all ✅ confirmed data
  - Reasoning provided for all ⚠️ inferred data
  - Explanation provided for all ❌ gaps

- [ ] **Actionable Next Steps**: Discovery questions prepared for information gaps

## Lead Quality Scoring Guide

After completing research, assign a lead quality score:

### A+ (Exceptional)
- All key information confirmed (✅)
- Multiple decision makers identified
- Clear pain points or opportunities visible
- Volume indicators suggest >10K shipments/month

### A (Strong)
- Most key information confirmed
- Primary decision maker identified
- Some pain points visible
- Volume indicators suggest 5K-10K shipments/month

### B+ (Good)
- Core information confirmed
- Decision maker identified
- Potential opportunities visible
- Volume indicators suggest 1K-5K shipments/month

### B (Fair)
- Basic information confirmed
- Contact information found
- Generic opportunities
- Volume unknown or <1K shipments/month

### C (Weak)
- Limited information available
- Unclear decision maker
- Low volume indicators or misaligned with FirstMile services

## Template Usage Notes

**When to Use This Template**:
- All new Brand Scout automated research leads
- Competitive intelligence on existing customers
- Market research for new verticals

**When NOT to Use This Template**:
- Inbound leads (use standard discovery process)
- Existing customer expansion (use customer success documentation)
- Partner/referral introductions (use relationship-based approach)

**Template Maintenance**:
- Review quarterly for field updates
- Update confidence level definitions as needed
- Add new research sources as they become available
