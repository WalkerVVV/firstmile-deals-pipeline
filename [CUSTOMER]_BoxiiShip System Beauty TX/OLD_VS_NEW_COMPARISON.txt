================================================================================

Formula: Stop-The-Clock Events / Total Shipments √ó 100

Breakdown:
  Successful Deliveries: ~16,800 (delivered to recipient)
  Exceptions:              ~700 (notice left, bad address, etc.)
  Stop-The-Clock Total:  ~17,500 (FirstMile job complete)
  In-Transit:              ~600 (still moving)
  Pending:                  ~86 (no events yet)
  Total:                  18,186

Calculation: 17,500 / 18,186 = 96.2%

What it means:
  - Of all shipments, 96.2% reached final disposition
  - 3.8% still in progress (in-transit + pending)
  - This is a COMPLETION metric, aligned with FirstMile standards

Why it's the primary metric:
  ‚úÖ Answers "Did FirstMile complete the delivery job?"
  ‚úÖ Includes late deliveries (job was still completed)
  ‚úÖ Aligned with FirstMile SLA reporting standards
  ‚úÖ Separates successful (96%) from exceptions (4%)
